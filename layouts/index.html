🏡 About me

I am Ivan Martinović, a researcher working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and medicine, based between Montenegro, US, and Canada. I hold a PhD in Biomedical Engineering, where my research focused on AI-based automatic analysis of fluoroscopic X-rays. This work was conducted in collaboration with the University of Toronto and the University of Pittsburgh under the supervision of Prof. Ervin Sejdić .

In parallel with my research, I co-founded epiSTEMe Hub , an organization dedicated to advancing scientific research in Montenegro and popularizing science and STEM education. Among my key achievements is Cetinje Smart City, a concept I developed that became Montenegro's first smart city initiative and received support from the International Telecommunication Union ( ITU).
